The Amazing World of Gumball

Frequently Asked Questions

Is it the serie finiched?

Unfortunately for all of us, the answer is yes. However, this does not detract from the fact that we may have different spin-offs or even movies in the future.

How many episodes does it has?

The Amazing World of Gumball has a total of 240 episodes divided into 6 seasons. It also has 14 more episodes divided into 2 specials that were aired in December 2019 and in October 2020.

Where can I watch it?

Nowadays it is still been played on some TV channels, you can search on your daily use "kids channels" to see if you are lucky. If you are not lucky, you can still pay for an online streaming service, there are many services wich have the full serie to watch. Some of the most famous ones are:

  • Sky Go
  • HBO Max
  • Amazon Video
  • Hulu
  • Google Play

Are there any movies about the serie?

Not yet. However, a film is has being announced to be in development and it is expected to be released in this comming 2023. Its official title at the momment is "The Amazing World of Gumball: The Movie!" (not a very original title I have to say).

Can I play any game about it?

Yes, there are many online webpages that provide games about this show, you can find some on the Resources page of this website.

Who is the autor of the serie?

Ben Bocquelet created this show for Cartoon Network in 2009.
